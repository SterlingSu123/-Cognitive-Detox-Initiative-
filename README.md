# Cognitive Detox Initiative | 认知禁毒行动

## 核心宣言

**杀死受害者的不是霸凌，是他们脑子里的毒。而你脑子里的毒，正在等着下一个你。**

What kills victims isn't bullying, but the poison in their minds. And that poison is waiting for the next you.

---

## 1. 我们为何在此 | Why We‘re Here

不是悼念，是解剖。  
不是流泪，是动手。  
不是又一个“反对霸凌”的口号仓库，是把 **“受害者”** 这三个字从你脑子里挖出去的手术台。

This is not a memorial. This is an autopsy.  
No tears. No slogans against bullying.  
This is the operating table where we cut the word **“victim”** out of your brain.

---

## 2. 核心问题 | The Core Problem

每一个被霸凌致死的人，都有同一个死因：

- 他们认同了那套用来审判他们的规则  
- 他们把价值评判权交给了外界  
- 他们错过了那个本可以翻盘的关键点  
- 他们潜意识里，已经不再相信自己值得活  

Everyone who was bullied to death died the same death:

- They internalized the rules used to judge them  
- They handed the keys to their self-worth to others  
- They missed the critical moment that could have turned everything around  
- Subconsciously, they no longer believed they deserved to live  

**霸凌只是递刀的人。**  
**真正的凶手，长在他们脑子里。**

The bullies just handed them the knife.  
The real killer lived in their own mind.

---

## 3. 我们做什么 | What We Do

**“认知禁毒”** 不是心理咨询，不是心灵鸡汤，不是“抱抱你”。是：

✅ 拆解受害者心态如何让人自动放弃手中的牌  
✅ 还原关键点——那些本可以翻盘、却被错过的一瞬间  
✅ 解剖死亡模型——上一个怎么死的，下一个会怎么死  
✅ 提供解毒方案——你手里的牌，怎么看见，怎么打  
✅ 收录活样板——那些没被吞掉的人，她们做对了什么  

Cognitive Detox is NOT therapy. NOT inspiration. NOT “you're not alone.”  
It IS:

- Dismantling **'victim mentality'**—how it makes you fold before the game even starts  
- Mapping **'critical moments'**—the turning points that could have changed everything  
- Anatomizing the **'death model'**—how the last one died, how the next one will die  
- Providing **'detox protocols'**—how to see the cards in your hand, how to play them  
- Archiving **'living proof'**—those who didn't get swallowed, and what they did right  

---

## 4. 我们不做什么 | What We DON'T Do

❌ 不骂霸凌者（他们只是重大工程事故的实习生，我们要抓总工程师）  
❌ 不消费眼泪（滥情是帮凶）  
❌ 不简化叙事（没有“纯洁受害者”，只有复杂的人）  
❌ 不代替你选择（只递牌，打不打在你）  

We DON'T:

- Blame the bullies (they're just the interns in a major engineering accident; we're after the chief engineer)  
- Milk your tears (sentimentality is complicity)  
- Simplify the story (no “innocent victims”—just complex humans)  
- Choose for you (we hand you the cards—you play them)

## 5. 核心命题 | Core Theses

- **受害者手里一直有牌**——只是没看见，或者看见了没打  
  The victim always had cards in their hand—they just didn't see them, or saw them and didn't play them  

- **断供权是最大的牌**——掌握对方需要的东西，你就不是弱者  
  The power to cut supply is the strongest card—if you have what they need, you're not weak  

- **关键点是被挖出来的，也是被创造出来的**——想活的人，会刨出生路  
  Critical moments are dug up—and created—those who want to live, carve out a path  

- **不许白死**——死者的任务，是拦住下一个  
  Don't die in vain—the dead's mission is to stop the next one  

- **霸凌只是递刀的，真正的凶手在你脑子里**  
  Bullies just hand you the knife—the real killer lives in your mind
